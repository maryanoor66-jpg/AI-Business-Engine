## What It Does

Most organizations run 5–20 projects simultaneously with no real-time intelligence on where things are breaking. This tool gives any executive or project manager an AI Chief Operating Officer in their browser — no server, no setup, no cost.

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 📊 Portfolio Dashboard | Live health scores, risk alerts, and burn rate for every project |

| 🔮 What-If Simulator | Model the impact of team size, scope, and timeline changes instantly |
| 🤖 AI COO Chat | Ask business questions, get COO-level answers backed by your data |

## Why I Built This

As a Project Manager who has delivered PKR 500M+ infrastructure portfolios, I experienced firsthand how much decision-making time is wasted on manual reporting, fragmented data, and reactive risk management.

AI COO solves three specific problems:

- **No real-time portfolio visibility** — most PMs still use static spreadsheets
- **Risk is detected too late** — by the time a report is ready, the damage is done
- **Executives can't ask questions of their data** — this lets them just type and get answers

This tool is the foundation of a broader SaaS product — **PM-Proof** — focused on AI governance and compliance for enterprise project managers.
